# 💫 About Me:
I’m a passionate Computer Science undergraduate specializing in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Lords Institute of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad. My interests lie at the intersection of AI and full-stack web development, and I thrive on building practical tech solutions that make a real-world impact.<br><br>I’ve developed projects like an AI-powered Face Recognition Attendance System, Brain Stroke Detection using CT scans, and a Kidney Disease Prediction System using machine learning and Python. Through internships and roles like Python Programming Intern at CodSoft and AI Intern at RAM Innovative Infotech, I’ve gained hands-on experience with Python, Flask, OpenCV, and SQLite.<br><br>In parallel, I actively contribute to the student tech ecosystem as the Graphics Team Member at IEEE LIET, and HR & Documentation Head at Artigenz LIET.
